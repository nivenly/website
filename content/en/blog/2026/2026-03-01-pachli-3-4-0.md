Pachli 3.4.0 has been released, and includes:

- Showing "hidden" hashtags on posts.
- A new search filter, to search for posts that do / do not quote other posts.
- Multiple bug fixes for quotes.
- Fixes for broken behaviour from different Android device vendors.

See the [full Pachli 3.4.0 release post](https://pachli.app/pachli/2026/02/27/3.4.0-release.html?utm_medium=referral&utm_source=nivenly.org&utm_campaign=release-3.4.0) for more details on these and other changes.
