Support for Mastodon's "[quote posts](https://blog.joinmastodon.org/2025/09/introducing-quote-posts/)" feature has landed in Pachli 3.2.0. You can now:

- Create posts that quote other posts.
- Set your policy for who can quote your posts (globally, and on a per-post basis).
- View quotes in posts that contain them.
- Receive and respond to notifications when other people quote your posts.
- Remove quotes of your posts from other people's posts.

When replying to posts or @-mentioning accounts in posts you are writing, Pachli 3.2.0 shows you the pronouns of the original poster. You can extend this feature to show pronouns throughout the UI, or disable the feature if your language does not have gendered pronouns.

See the [full Pachli 3.2.0 release post](https://pachli.app/pachli/2025/11/30/3.2.0-release.html?utm_medium=referral&utm_source=nivenly.org&utm_campaign=release-3.2.0) for more details on these and other changes.
